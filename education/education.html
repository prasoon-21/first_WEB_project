B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Roorkee 

 
Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IIT Roorkee) is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering institutes in India. Established in 1847 as Thomason College of Civil Engineering, it was later renamed IIT Roorkee in 2001 when it was made an IIT (Indian Institute of Technology), which are autonomous public technical universities in India.
Key Information about IIT Roorkee:
Location: Roorkee, Uttarakhand, India
Type: Public technical university
Institute Code: IITR
Founder: Sir James Thomason (established as Thomason College of Civil Engineering)
Academic Programs: IIT Roorkee offers undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech, M.Sc.), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs across various engineering, science, and management disciplines.
